Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions and interactive installations, we invite visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we experience art.

Our rotating collections and curated exhibitions span the full spectrum of digital expression — from generative art and algorithmic design to augmented reality and machine-learning installations. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, MonoMuse offers new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity in everyone who walks through our doors.
